About Me

My name is Alain Christe and I was born on May 11, 1998 in Woodstock, Illinois. I am a Swiss-American dual citizen and undergraduate CS major at the University of Iowa. Like many others, I've spent the majority of my life messing with technology, from dial-up internet to overclocking to building the next generation of software.

I like to spend my free time traveling the most, a gift given to me by my parents along with the most love and support anyone could ask for. It is thanks to the family, friends, and colleagues in my life that I feel so driven to help others and make life easier.

From the moment I learned to code, I was amazed by the power to create something out of nothing and the ability to change the world. In a time when we still reckon with war and inequality, I believe that machine intelligence and human compassion together hold the key to a better future.
